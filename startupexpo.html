About the Event.


STARTUP EXPO

Startup Expo 2019 is a one-day event hosted by IEDC, enabling a platform for entrepreneurs to gain exposure to the rapidly maturing startup ecosystem in India. Startups from around the nation get an opportunity to showcase their ideas, products and services to a wide range of audience consisting of prominent investors, professionals, companies, expert personnels, etc.


The aim of this event is to bring a pool of resources required by young budding startups to a single platform so that they can interact with these resource personnels and procure the necessary investment for their further development and growth.


The expo arena will have stalls available on rent. The expo will run parallel to the other events of EDVENTURE'19.


Why should you be a part of the Expo?

  • Exhibiting to targeted visitors through direct face-to-face meetings is one of the most effective and efficient marketing strategies to build brand equity.
  • Generate lucrative, highly targeted business leads by interacting face-to-face with potential investors /incubators/accelerators.
  • Great opportunity to learn from the feedback of end users to determine and focus action.
  • Develop and strengthen brand image and create lasting impression through exclusive coverage .
  • Network with entrepreneurs and investors as the expo is a cost-effective networking and advertising medium

Stall Sizes

  • Option 1 – 1.5 x3 Sq Mtr Stall space-rate: 1000/-
  • Option 2 – 3 x3 Sq Mtr Stall space-rate: 2000/-

Rules

Stall Owners – Only the registered team of 3 participants will be allowed in the stall. No “Booth Buddies” i.e. family, friends etc. who are not registered to attend, are allowed.


Payment Terms

– All payments are required to be made online while registering for the event on http://www.edventure19.in/
Cancellation Policy – Any cancellation needs to be made in writing addressing the organizer as given below:

  • 25% refund will be processed if cancellation request is made

In the event of cancellation of the contract, for whatever reasons or in case of any dispute, a mutual decision will be taken in agreement with the Organizer.

Obligations

  • Not Permitted – Liquor, Illegal Substances, Material that is disrespectful/ demeaning to other brands
  • Responsibility – It is your responsibility as the owner of the stalls to provide information to the visitors regarding your company/product/service. You shall not leave the stalls unattended and keep it fully staffed at all times. You shall keep the walkways clear from your materials and keep the stall area clean and tidy. Appropriate business etiquette shall be maintained.
  • No Disturbance – You shall not play videos or music at volume levels that can be disturbing the other stalls.

Allotment

  • Allocation – The allotment of the stalls will be on a first-come-first basis.
  • Alteration to the Floor Plan – The organizers reserve the right to make any changes to the floorplan as required.

Pack-In and Pack-Out

  • Setup Time – All start-ups will be allowed 3 hours’ time to setup their stalls before the event starts.
  • Pack up Time – All start-ups are required to close by 6 pm on either days of the event.
  • Materials – All banners/boards and other collateral, display products etc. charges will be borne by the individual start-up including their transport to and from the event venue.
