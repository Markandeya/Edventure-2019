About Edventure.


Edventure19 is an event spanning three days, organized by the Innovation and Entrepreneurship development cell of College of Engineering Trivandrum to bring together a dynamic group of thinkers and doers in a provocative setting. It hosts an array of activities and contests that will exemplify the holistic world view and ideas of participants capable of positively impacting our planet in new ways.

Edventure19 provides a platform to initiate new connections, collaborations and progress among pioneers and aspirants of the entrepreneur community whose future glitters with extraordinary promise.

IEDC at CET.

The Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Cell (IEDC) is an active student-run cell under College of Engineering Trivandrum Centre for Interdisciplinary Research (CCIR) that seeks to create and promote innovation and entrepreneurship skills among the students of CET. It acts to foster innovative thinking and is a facilitator between the students and the industries outside by acting as a hub of contacts that helps the wannabe entrepreneurs in finding the right people to approach for any task.
