About Edventure.


Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Cell of CET (IEDC-CET) brings to you the 2nd edition of Edventure series, Edventure'19, in collaboration with the Kerala Startup Mission (KSUM), Trest Park, Kerala State Council for Science, Technology and Environment (KSCSTE) and the CET Alumni Association (CETAA).

It is an event spanning three days to bring together thinkers and doers in a provocative setting with events such as the Startup Expo, Project Expo, Talk Series and a lot more!

The mission of the Edventure'19 is to establish and roll out a continuing process of empowering entrepreneurs and startups by providing the right infusion of exposure, networking, support and inspiration. Edventure 19 aims at creating a flourishing startup ecosystem in CET for a dynamic student community.

It is certainly a force to reckon with for budding entrepreneurs at all levels to exemplify the holistic worldview and positively impact our planet in new ways.

IEDC at CET.

The Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Cell (IEDC) is an active student-run cell under College of Engineering Trivandrum Centre for Interdisciplinary Research (CCIR) that seeks to create and promote innovation and entrepreneurship skills among the students of CET. It acts to foster innovative thinking and is a facilitator between the students and the industries outside by acting as a hub of contacts that helps the wannabe entrepreneurs in finding the right people to approach for any task.
