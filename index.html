About Edventure.

Edventure19 is an event spanning three days, organized by the Innovation and Entrepreneurship development cell of College of Engineering Trivandrum to bring together a dynamic group of thinkers and doers in a provocative setting. It hosts an array of activities and contests that will exemplify the holistic world view and ideas of participants capable of positively impacting our planet in new ways.

Edventure19 provides a platform to initiate new connections, collaborations and progress among pioneers and aspirants of the entrepreneur community whose future glitters with extraordinary promise.

Events, Workshops et al.

Talk Series

The talk series brings together a true cross section of trailblazers in our society, each with their own unique journey and stories to share. Fuel your motivation by hearing the incredible accounts of struggle and triumph and from the protagonists themselves! Edventure 19 has lined up an outstanding and diverse slate of speakers who will undoubtedly spark deep discussion and connection with their audience.

  • Learn more
Workshops

Dream of taking on the world, but feel ill-equipped for it? All one needs to feel prepared and confident enough to turn this dream to reality is to expose themselves to new opportunities, discover their special talents and capabilities, and gain expertise in them. Realize your potential and spend just a small amount of your time in developing your skills by joining one (or more!) of our workshops conducted by the experts. Who knows, it may open doors to exciting opportunities and possibilities in your future!

  • Learn more
Competitions

The best entrepreneurs are not necessarily the most informed, but they are certainly the most competitive. An array of competitions allow you to put your competence and entrepreneurial prowess to test. Maximise your engagement and growth hand-in-hand by taking part in the competitions that could perhaps initiate the spark that transforms into your startup. Exciting prizes await you!

  • Learn more
Conclave

Learning from the experienced is vital when you are just starting out and venturing into relatively new fields of interest. At the Conclave, you will get the chance to gain the knowledge and clues to succeeding from some of the most esteemed alumni of the College of Engineering Trivandrum who have made their mark in their respective fields.

  • Learn more
Project Expo

Edventure19's keystone event, the project/product expo is an unforgettable gathering of brilliant ideas and their ideators. While being an arena for entrepreneurs to showcase their efforts, it is also a great place to catch up with today's groundbreaking technologies and the business models built on them. There is a lot that can be learnt from their innovative power and inventive spirit.

  • Learn more
Startup Expo

You can give way to tremendous opportunities by giving a small kickstart to that one great idea. The Start-up Expo gives that major opportunity to budding and aspiring entrepreneurs and innovators by providing them the platform to showcase their pioneering ideas and projects. Make the most of this chance to meet investors, pitch them your top-notch ideas, raise funds and also receive some helpful advice from the experts.

  • Learn more
Panel Disussions

Conversation and discussion plays a valuable role in our decisions, connections and innovations. It can strengthen our beliefs and change our perspectives. The panel discussion brings together experts who are inspired to discuss their views on some of the society's most pressing challenges with their peers in the community. Through this discussion, we can guarantee thrilling glimpses of the landscape ahead.

  • Learn more
RoundTable Talks

“While it is wise to learn from experience, it is wiser to learn from the experiences of others.”- Rick Warren Broaden your perspective and knowledge through the conversations with competent and accomplished entrepreneurs. These are the people who are masters of their craft and are brimming with a myriad of insightful ideas, knowledge and experiences. So, just don’t be afraid to question and speak your mind!

  • Learn more
Bootcamp Summit

Being connected to people on the same journey as us is key to keeping ourselves encouraged and on track in achieving our goals. The Bootcamp is the perfect opportunity to form and be a part of a supportive network of people, where we bring together the IEDC cells from various colleges at one spot. Let’s connect, exchange ideas, expand our horizons a bit, and create some memorable moments with like-minded people.

  • Learn more
Spill the Beans

What if we told you that you could have your favorite seniors casually explain the nitty gritty of the business while chilling on bean bags? Well, we can arrange just that for you, except that your favorite seniors are also pioneers of some of the most successful entrepreneurial ventures. Here's an opportunity to make meaningful and uninhibited conversations with pathbreakers. Starting these conversations could build relationships that continue to flourish well after this event.
