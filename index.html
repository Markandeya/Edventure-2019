About E-NOVUS


Enovus, the flagship event of IEDC-CET is an entrepreneurship summit which brings together entrepreneurs and experts from various fields to share the experiences that moulded them to their level of dexterity and finesse.

College of Engineering Trivandrum (CET) has been one of the few Govt institute in Kerala trying to build the entrepreneurship & start-up ecosystem through the MHRD initiatives and Innovation and Entrepreneurship and Development Cells (IEDC) under the Kerala Startup Mission.

Every year CET hosts an E-Summit and this year, it has been named E-NOVUS, to promote the spirit of entrepreneurship among the youth of the country. This year, E-NOVUS will be hosted at CET during FEB 29 to MARCH 1 , 2020. As part of E-NOVUS this year, we are proud to launch the first edition of the Entrepreneurship Awareness Programme (EAP).


HIGHLIGHT OF THE EVENT.

Entrepreneurship Awareness Program

EAP is a one-of-a-kind accelerator program aimed at fostering entrepreneurship talent among participants and providing them with a holistic overview of the start-up ecosystem.

IEDC at CET.

The Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Cell (IEDC) is an active student-run cell under College of Engineering Trivandrum Centre for Interdisciplinary Research (CCIR) that seeks to create and promote innovation and entrepreneurship skills among the students of CET. It acts to foster innovative thinking and is a facilitator between the students and the industries outside by acting as a hub of contacts that helps the wannabe entrepreneurs in finding the right people to approach for any task.
