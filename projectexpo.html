PROJECT EXPO.


"Necessity is The Mother Of Inventions"

Is your mind grappling with a life changing idea that people are craving for? Get your tools ready and present your idea in the realised form of a product in front of eminent judges.Present your projects at the Project Expo 19 , conducted by IEDC-CET' Edventure'19.Project Expo will be held in association with Kerala Startup Mission (KSUM). Judging panel consists of pioneers and entrepreneurs , who are a part of KSUM. Showcase your project and get the chance to be the winners.

Project Expo is an exhibition that provides all students a chance to display their dream projects and learn about different ideas that can fuel their startup dreams.
Project Expo is conducted on 12th May under two categories, separately for CETians and non-CETians. Non-CETian teams could be from technical schools or polytechnic colleges. There will be a separate forenoon session and an afternoon session. Exciting prizes await the winning teams and the best team will gain direct entry to the finals of Idea Grant by KSUM.

Renowned companies and entrepreneurs will be present to witness the demonstration of your ideas' prototype.The Project expo provides a platform for showcasing your ideas and also kickstart your career in business if your idea clicks with the judging panel.

Rules

♦️Each Team should have a maximum of 5 members.
♦️ Team Members should submit their institute ID cards.
♦️Screening based on the abstract filled in the Google form.
♦️The judging criteria are:Innovation, Feasibility, Business plan of the project.
♦️All teams need to showcase their prototypes of their projects.

Registration fee- Rs 100 per head.

Prizes worth Rs. 15k.
